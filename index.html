Meet Sharla

Thirty Years of Transformations

Sharla Mohney started at the Vidal Sassoon Academy in California. What followed was three decades of relentless education, precision craft, and the kind of client loyalty you only earn when you consistently deliver on your promise.

She is certified in four extension methods and has deep expertise in multicultural hair textures - meaning she can work with any hair type that walks through her door. She selects premium professional products, researches formulas, and shows up prepared for every single appointment.

Beyond the salon, her work has been featured in national print and advertising campaigns for American Express and Shinola Detroit, and she has been a trusted collaborator at Detroit Fashion Week for over fifteen years.

"Sharla's talent truly shines as she effortlessly blends creativity with precision. Her attention to detail and dedication to her skill are unparalleled. Thanks to Sharla, our projects have been accepted in highly acclaimed publications with notable mentions on quality."

Holly Q. - Publisher, IKONIK Magazine
