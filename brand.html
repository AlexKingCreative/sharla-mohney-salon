05 / Tone of Voice

Brand Voice

Confident, precise, and warm. Sharla's brand speaks like an expert who has nothing to prove - because after 30 years, she doesn't. Copy leads with the client's outcome, not the stylist's credentials. It is specific, never vague. It is professional, never cold.
